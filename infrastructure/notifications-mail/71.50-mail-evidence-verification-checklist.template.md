# 71.50 Mail Evidence Retention Verification Checklist

- Central logs enforce retention lifecycle expiry on schedule.
- SMTP relay logs rotate and purge per configured retention window.
- Evidence-Pack receipts are retained per linked document retention class.
- Legal hold by correlation_id prevents purge for held evidence.
- Purge operations emit PurgeStarted and PurgeCompleted audit events.
- Central logs do not retain email bodies or token-bearing links.
