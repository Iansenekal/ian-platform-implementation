# 40.30 n8n Secret Exposure Incident Response

1. Contain: disable exposed credential/service account immediately.
2. Assess: identify exposure locations (logs, git, ticketing, screenshots).
3. Rotate: issue replacement credential and update n8n credential binding.
4. Purge: remove leaked material from stores and histories where possible.
5. Review: analyze workflow executions during exposure window.
6. Prevent: apply control improvements and scanning/training updates.
