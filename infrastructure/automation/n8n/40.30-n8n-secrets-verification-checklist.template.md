# 40.30 n8n Secrets and Credentials Verification Checklist

- No secrets appear in compose files, repository files, docs, or exported workflow JSON.
- Host-level secret files exist under /opt/n8n/secrets with required permissions.
- n8n encryption key is configured and credential storage is encrypted at rest.
- Credential inventory exists and matches active credential set.
- Least-privilege tests confirm blocked operations fail.
- Rotation schedule exists and recent rotation evidence is recorded.
- Secret-exposure incident tabletop has been completed.
