# 41.30 Secret Exposure Incident Response (Windmill)

1. Contain: disable exposed credential and pause impacted jobs.
2. Assess: identify leak location and affected integrations.
3. Rotate: issue replacement credential and update secret references.
4. Purge: remove leaked material from logs/repos/artifacts.
5. Review: audit job executions during the exposure window.
6. Prevent: update controls, scanning rules, and RBAC where needed.
