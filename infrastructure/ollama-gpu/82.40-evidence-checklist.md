- `ollama --version`
- `systemctl status ollama --no-pager`
- `ss -tulpen | grep 11434`
- `curl -s http://127.0.0.1:11434/api/tags | jq .`
- Remote caller proof from `hai-backend01` (or approved caller host)
- `ufw status verbose` and `ufw status numbered`
- GPU checks: `lspci -k`, `/dev/dri`, `/dev/kfd`, `rocm-smi` (if installed)
- `/etc/systemd/system/ollama.service.d/override.conf`

Store evidence under the pre-phase compliance pack aligned to `06.40` retention rules.
