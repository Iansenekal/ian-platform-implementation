# 70.30 Signature Verification Checklist

- Receipt exists in Evidence-Pack for approved document version.
- Document SHA-256 matches `document_sha256` in receipt.
- Receipt signature verifies with trusted public key.
- Signer subject and step match expected approval-chain assignment.
- Audit trail confirms MFA and approval timestamp integrity.
- Historical receipts remain verifiable after key rotation.
- Post-approval document tamper test produces hash mismatch.
